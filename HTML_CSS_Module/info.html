Ada Lovelace

Info about The Icon

  • Full Name : Augusta Ada King-Noel, Countess of Lovelace
  • Born : December 10, 1815
  • Known for : First computer algorithm
  • Worked on : Analytical Engine with Charles Babbage

Ada's Poetical Science

Born to the iconic poet Lord Byron and Lady Byron, Ada Lovelace inherited both intellect and imagination.
Though her father was known for his verses, Ada channeled her creativity into numbers and logic.
“Imagination is the Discovering Faculty, pre-eminently. It is that which penetrates into the unseen worlds around us...”
— Ada Lovelace

She famously described her approach to the Engine as a blend of science and art, calling it a“Poetical Science.”

Quote by the Countess

"Your best and wisest refuge from all troubles is in your science." – Ada Lovelace
